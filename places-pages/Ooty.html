Doddabetta Peak Ooty, Tamil Nadu

Overview

Located 10 kilometres from Ooty, Doddabetta Peak is set at an altitude of 8,606 feet, and is the highest point of South India.

Suggested Duration For Exploring :

Minimum 1 hour

What To Expect?

Surrounded by forested areas, the view of Nilgiris from the Doddabetta Peak is just breath-taking! Visit the Telescope House at the top for more spectacular panoramic views of the surroundings.

Tips :

  • You can visit the peak between 8:30 a.m. and 6:30 p.m. daily.
  • Wear comfortable sneakers to explore the entire area.
  • The entry fee is Rs. 10 per person.

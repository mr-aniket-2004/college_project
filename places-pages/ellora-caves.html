Ellora Caves , Aurangabad

Overview

Featuring Buddhist, Hindu and Jain monuments, Ellora Caves are a stunning rock-cut temple complex with 34 monasteries and temples. It takes about an hour by road to reach Ellora from Aurangabad. The UNESCO website says that Ellora 'illustrates the spirit of tolerance that was characteristic of ancient India'.

Suggested Duration For Exploring :

Around 3 -4 Hours

What To Expect?

Witness marvellous craftsmanship at these multi religious caves that jut out of ancient rocks, marvel at the architectural genius of the Kailash Temple and be awestruck by stories of a flourishing Indian civilization that dates back to AD 600 - 1000.

Tips :

  • Be a responsible tourist and do not vandalise the ancient sites.
  • The surroundings provide for some unique backgrounds which you can put to good use in photography sessions.
  • Abstain from using plastic, as it is a sensitive zone and demands utmost precaution with so many tourists flocking here every day.

Baga Beach , Goa

Overview

One of the most popular beaches in Goa, Baga Beach offers quirky beach-side shacks and a number of thrilling water sports.

Suggested Duration For Exploring :

Around 2 - 3 Hours

What To Expect?

Walk along the golden shores or indulge in water sports like parasailing, banana rides and jet skiing. As the sun goes down, the beachside clubs come alive with groovy beats and peppy performances.

Tips :

  • Do not venture into the sea before consulting the lifeguard on duty, especially during the monsoons.
  • Before stepping out in the sun, don’t forget to apply sunscreen on the exposed areas of your skin.
  • Don’t forget to check out Tito’s Lane if you want to indulge in a shopping spree.
